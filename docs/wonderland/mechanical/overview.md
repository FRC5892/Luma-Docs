# Wonderland: Mechanical

## What Mechanical Does

Mechanical builds, assembles, and maintains the robot's physical systems so they stay reliable under match stress.

Mechanical keeps the robot reliable under stress and reduces avoidable post-match repairs.

Core responsibilities include:

- Structural integrity and mechanism durability
- Fastener retention and threadlocker process discipline
- Rapid pit repairs and between-match inspections
- Verification that mechanical changes preserve performance
