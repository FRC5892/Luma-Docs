# Wonderland: Scouting and Strategy

## What Scouting and Strategy Does

Scouting and Strategy converts match data into decisions for alliance planning, matchup selection, and playoff preparation.

Scouting and strategy should be evidence-driven, current, and directly tied to match decisions. Wrong assumptions usually collapse in front of everyone.

Core responsibilities include:

- Capturing high-value match observations
- Building reliable picklist inputs
- Tracking opponent and partner tendencies
- Running quick debrief loops after each match
