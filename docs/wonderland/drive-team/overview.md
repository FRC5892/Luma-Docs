# Wonderland: Drive Team

## What Drive Team Does

Drive Team executes match strategy in real time and coordinates communication between operators, coach, and pit.

Drive team execution translates strategy into consistent in-match decisions under pressure.

Core responsibilities include:

- Fast in-match decision making
- Clear communication under pressure
- Timeout triage and fallback planning
- Immediate post-match feedback loops
