# Wonderland: Electrical

## What Electrical Does

Electrical designs and maintains power, wiring, and signal infrastructure so the robot remains safe, stable, and match-ready.

Electrical keeps power, wiring, and signaling reliable through repeatable process and verification.

Core responsibilities include:

- Battery health and competition battery lifecycle tracking
- Reliable power distribution and connector integrity
- CAN and control wiring quality
- Pit charging workflows and electrical fault isolation
